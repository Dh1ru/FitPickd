Our Story

From Guwahati to your wardrobe

The FitPickd Journey

FitPickd was born from a simple yet powerful belief: every man deserves to feel confident in what he wears. Founded in the vibrant city of Guwahati, our journey began with a vision to create premium men's clothing that combines style, comfort, and quality.

We understand that clothing is more than just fabric - it's an expression of who you are. That's why we source the finest materials from across India, working with skilled artisans and premium suppliers to ensure every piece meets our high standards.

Our Mission

To provide men with clothing that not only looks exceptional but feels even better. We believe in creating pieces that become a part of your identity, helping you own every room you walk into.

Our Values

  • Quality First: Every piece is crafted with attention to detail
  • Style Meets Comfort: We never compromise on either
  • Authenticity: Be true to yourself, be FitPickd
  • Community: Building a community of confident, stylish men

Rooted in Guwahati

Our story begins in the heart of Northeast India - Guwahati. This vibrant city, known for its rich culture and warm hospitality, has shaped our brand's identity and values.

From Guwahati, we've expanded our reach across India, sourcing the finest materials and working with skilled artisans from different regions. This diversity in sourcing allows us to offer unique pieces that reflect the rich tapestry of Indian craftsmanship.

Why Guwahati?

Guwahati represents the perfect blend of tradition and modernity. It's a city that embraces change while staying true to its roots - exactly what we aim to achieve with our clothing. The city's entrepreneurial spirit and growing fashion consciousness make it the ideal birthplace for FitPickd.
